Instructions More about project
Encryption: Enter your text in field 'Original text' and enter your key. Choose encryption (if it's not already chosen) and click button 'Start'.
Decryption: Enter your text in field 'Encrypted text'. Don't enter key. Choose decryption and click button 'Start'.
If you want to load text from file, please choose if you want to do encryption or decryption so that it is loaded into the correct field.
Note: You have to use English alphabet in order to make the algorithm work successfully. Also, please don't use keys longer than 1/10 of the length of the original text.
